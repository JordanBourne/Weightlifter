Ice Cream Fitness
A simple workout routine for beginners that is based on Starting Strength, but with some isolation movements accessories added in for extra size gains. This is for people who already have a little experience in the gym and with a barbell, and are looking for a more complete program. Program originates from Muscle and Strength.
